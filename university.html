Introduction

ROI & The Business Case

Before you spend a dollar on ads, you need to understand the math. This is not about hope — it is about knowing your numbers so every dollar you invest has a predictable return.

The average producing agent brings in $15,000 to $40,000+ in company dollar (split/fees) to your brokerage per year. If you recruit just one producing agent per month from your attraction system, your annual office revenue increases dramatically. Your ad spend to generate that recruit is typically between $500 and $1,500 when the system is optimized.

The question is never "should I spend money on ads?" The question is "what is my cost to acquire a producing agent, and is it less than their first-year company dollar?" Once you know that number, you scale it.

The goal: Get your cost per recruit under $1,500. The LTV (Lifetime Value) of a producing agent makes this the highest ROI investment in your business.

Introduction

Thesis-Led Recruiting

Most office owners recruit by cold calling agents and begging them to get coffee. They compete on split and fees — the worst possible position. We don't do that. We use Thesis-Led Recruiting.

Instead of chasing agents, you run ads presenting a specific "Thesis" — a logical argument for how an agent can grow their business by plugging into your systems (your value stack). You capture their attention with a Video Sales Letter (VSL), nurture them with automated emails and texts, and they book a call with you when they are ready to move.

The strategy here is different. You attract agents by offering a solution to their biggest pain point (lead gen, admin, burnout). If they buy the thesis, they buy the brokerage.

Why this matters: You position yourself as a leader and a solution provider, not a beggar. The automated nurture sequence does the heavy lifting while you focus on running your office.

A2P SMS Registration

A2P (Application-to-Person) 10DLC is the telecom compliance standard that allows your GHL account to send automated SMS messages to leads. Without A2P approval, your texts will not be delivered. This takes approximately one week to be approved — apply immediately.

Do not launch ads until your A2P is approved. If leads come in and your texts are not delivering, you will lose them in the first 5 minutes.

How to Apply

  1. In GHL: Settings → Phone Numbers → Trust Center → A2P Wizard → Start registering now
  2. Brand Details: Select Low Volume Standard
  3. Campaign Use Case: Select Low Volume Mixed (Recommended)

Campaign Use Case Description (copy this exactly)

Overall SMS Use: Customer quality experience, improve business processes, and consented marketing.

Appointment Reminders: We utilise SMS as a primary mode of reminding customers about their upcoming appointments. Our automated system sends out messages 24, 12, and 1 hour before the scheduled appointment. The SMS includes details such as the date, time, location, and purpose of the appointment, reducing the likelihood of no-shows and late arrivals.

Sample SMS Messages (use these in the application)

Sample #1:
Hi [First Name], this is [Team Member Name] from [BUSINESS NAME]! We've received your request for further details and are looking forward to talking with you. You can schedule your meeting via this link: [Calendar Link]. Standard data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe.
Sample #2:
Hello [First Name], this is an automated message from [BUSINESS NAME]. Your form has been successfully submitted! One of our team members will reach out to you soon. Reply HELP for help, STOP to stop receiving messages. Data rates may apply.

Other Settings

  • Messages will include an embedded link: Yes
  • Messages will include phone numbers: Yes
  • Age-gated content: No
  • Direct lending content: No

Module 2

Your Offer & VSL

A great system with a weak offer will not convert. This module teaches you how to build a specific, compelling offer that makes buyers and sellers raise their hand — and how to communicate it through a Video Sales Letter.

The Offer Formula

Most agents market themselves with generic statements like "I am a local expert" or "I have 10 years of experience." This sounds like every other agent. A real offer is specific, promises a clear result, and removes a common frustration.

The Formula

I help [type of client] get [desirable result] without [common frustration] by using [your unique method].

Examples

  • "I help first-time buyers own a home with no down payment using a 3-step lender-approved strategy."
  • "I help downsizing seniors sell their homes in 14 days without endless showings using our Private Buyer Pool system."
  • "I help move-up buyers purchase their next home before selling their current one — without carrying two mortgages."

Pick one niche and one promise. You can always expand later. The agents who try to be everything to everyone convert the least.

Module 2

Buyer-Side Offers (All 12)

Choose ONE of these offers for your first ad campaign. Each offer has been tested and proven to generate buyer leads. Pick the one that best fits your market and your personality.

Offer 1: "See Homes in [City] Under $X"

Best for: High-volume markets with clear price points

A simple ad that gives buyers a free, verified list of homes under a set price. These are real, active MLS listings updated daily — not fake bait-and-switch listings.

Fulfillment: Check your CRM daily. When a lead comes in, ask their price range and preferred neighborhoods, then send 3–5 matching listings from your MLS. This is a real service, not a trick.

Offer 2: "Off-Market / Unlisted Properties"

Best for: Competitive markets where buyers are frustrated by losing bidding wars

Promises buyers access to homes not yet on Zillow or Realtor.com — off-market, Coming Soon, or pre-list properties shared quietly between agents.

Fulfillment: You do not need a secret list. You need speed. Send Coming Soon listings before they hit the public portals. Network with other agents for pre-list opportunities. The value is access and timing, not magic.

Offer 3: "Win Your Down Payment Back Challenge"

Best for: First-time buyer markets

A giveaway-style offer. Buyers enter to win their down payment back (up to $10,000) when they buy with you within 60 days. Everyone who does not win gets a smaller bonus like a home warranty.

Fulfillment: Run a quarterly drawing. Fulfill the smaller bonuses (home warranty, gift card) at closing out of your commission. Cap entries at 7 buyers per month to create urgency.

Offer 4: "Free Home Warranty + $1,000 Furniture Credit"

Best for: Move-in ready buyer market

A move-in bonus for buyers who choose you as their agent. Takes the sting out of closing costs and makes the decision feel more tangible.

Fulfillment: Purchase a home warranty ($400–$600) and provide a $1,000 gift card (Amazon, Home Depot, or VISA) at closing. Total cost: ~$1,500. Easily covered by your commission.

Offer 5: "Zero Stress Home Buyer Guarantee"

Best for: Agents with strong track records and high confidence

If a buyer purchases with you and is not happy with their home within 12 months, you will sell it for free (no listing-side commission). This is a bold guarantee that signals confidence.

Fulfillment: Set clear terms in writing. Most buyers who are happy with their purchase will never invoke this — but the guarantee alone increases conversion dramatically.

Offer 6: "Buy Now, Sell Later Bridge Program"

Best for: Move-up buyer market

Helps move-up buyers purchase their new home before their current one sells. If their old home does not sell within 90 days, you cover their first month's mortgage payment (up to a set amount).

Fulfillment: Partner with a bridge loan lender. Set a cap on your mortgage coverage commitment. This offer attracts highly motivated, high-value buyers.

Offer 7: "Price Drop Alert Service"

Best for: Buyers who are watching and waiting

A free text alert system that notifies buyers instantly when homes in their target area drop in price. Buyers sign up, you set up an MLS alert, and they get a text the moment a price reduction happens.

Fulfillment: Set up automated MLS alerts in your CRM. When a price drop fires, GHL sends the lead a text. Simple, automated, and highly appreciated.

Offer 8: "Home Buyer's Renovation Credit"

Best for: Fixer-upper or older home markets

A $1,000 post-closing credit for buyers to use toward repairs or upgrades. Removes the fear of buying a home that needs work.

Fulfillment: Provide a $1,000 check or gift card at closing. Partner with a local contractor to offer discounted services as an added bonus.

Offer 9: "Foreclosure & Bank-Owned List"

Best for: Value-focused buyer markets

A free, daily-updated list of homes priced below market value — foreclosures, bank-owned properties, and distressed sales. Attracts buyers who are motivated by deals.

Fulfillment: Pull foreclosure and REO listings from your MLS daily. Send the list as a PDF or direct MLS link. Update it every 24–48 hours to maintain credibility.

Offer 10: "Local Buyer Market Report"

Best for: Educating early-stage buyers

A monthly report showing local housing trends — average prices, days on market, inventory levels, and rate updates. Positions you as the local market expert.

Fulfillment: Pull data from your MLS monthly. Format it as a simple PDF or email. Use Canva to make it look professional. Send to your entire lead database.

Offer 11: "Homes by Monthly Payment"

Best for: Budget-conscious buyers who think in payments, not prices

Buyers tell you their ideal monthly payment, and you send them a list of homes that fit it based on current rates and down payment assumptions.

Fulfillment: Use a mortgage calculator to back into the purchase price from their target payment. Send matching listings. Partner with a lender to provide pre-approval support.

Offer 12: "Homes That Pay You Back"

Best for: Investor-minded buyers or high cost-of-living markets

A list of homes with rentable suites, basement apartments, or ADUs (accessory dwelling units) that generate rental income to offset the mortgage payment.

Fulfillment: Search your MLS for listings with secondary suites or legal rental units. Include estimated rental income and net mortgage cost in your list.

Seller-Side Offers (All 5)

If you are focusing on listings, choose one of these seller offers. Each one removes a specific fear or frustration that prevents sellers from taking action.

Offer 1: "Sell for Top Dollar or We Sell It for Free"

If the home sells below your recommended price range, you waive your listing commission. This signals extreme confidence in your pricing strategy and marketing.

How to protect yourself: Set the price range conservatively. If the home sells within your range, you earn your full commission. If it sells below, you waive the listing side only — the buyer's agent commission still applies.

Offer 2: "The Market Timing Guarantee"

If the local market drops by more than 5% before their home sells, you cut your commission in half. This removes the fear of listing at the wrong time.

How to protect yourself: Define "market drop" clearly in writing — use a specific index or average sale price metric. Set a time cap (e.g., within 90 days of listing).

Offer 3: "Skip the Hassle Buyout"

If the home does not sell within 60 days, you or an investor partner will purchase it at an agreed-upon price. Removes the fear of being stuck on the market forever.

How to protect yourself: Partner with a real estate investor or iBuyer program. Set the buyout price at 85–90% of market value. This is a safety net, not a primary exit.

Offer 4: "The Home Sale Timing Forecast"

A visual report that breaks down current prices, days on market, and seasonal trends to tell sellers exactly when to list for maximum return.

Fulfillment: Pull 90 days of sold data from your MLS. Create a simple chart showing average sale price by month. Present it as a free consultation deliverable.

Offer 5: "Sold Above List Case Study Report"

A report showing real homes in your city that sold above asking price — and exactly what those sellers did differently (staging, pricing strategy, marketing).

Fulfillment: Pull 5–10 recent above-asking sales from your MLS. Write a one-paragraph story for each. Format as a PDF. This doubles as a listing presentation tool.

Recording Your VSL

Your VSL is your 24/7 digital recruiter. It is a 5–15 minute video that presents your thesis, your value stack, and your call to action. It does the heavy lifting so your demo calls are just closing conversations.

You do not need a studio or professional editing skills. You need a quiet room, a webcam, and the Thesis Presentation slide deck we provide. Record it on Zoom or Loom while screen-sharing your slides.

The VSL Framework

  1. Hook: Call out your target agent and state the big promise.
  2. The Problem: Agitate the pain they are experiencing (burnout, no leads, split is too high).
  3. The Shift: Introduce the insight that changes everything (your value stack).
  4. Your Solution: Explain your system, your offer, and what makes your office different.
  5. The CTA: Tell them exactly what to do next (book a discovery call).

Live Example: See our model VSL at video.eliteagentblueprint.com/vsl to see exactly how this should look and sound.

Creating Eye-Catching AI Image Ads

High-quality ad images stop the scroll. AI image generators can produce professional visuals in minutes. You do not need to hire a designer for your first recruiting campaign.

Using AI to Generate Ad Images

Module 4

Conversion & The Nurture Machine

Generating leads is 10% of the battle. The other 90% is follow-up. This module gives you the exact scripts and automated sequences to turn cold internet leads into booked appointments — even the ones who are not ready today.

Speed to Lead

If you do not contact a lead within the first 5 minutes of them opting in, your chances of converting them drop by over 400%. The first text is automated through GHL — but when they reply, you need to be ready.

Automated Initial SMS (set this up in GHL Workflows)

Hey {First Name}, it's {Your Name} from {Business Name}. I just sent over the {Offer Name} you requested — did you receive it? 📋
If no reply after 2 hours:
Hi {First Name}, just following up to make sure you got the list I sent. Let me know if you have any questions — happy to help. — {Your Name}

When They Reply

The moment a lead replies to your text, jump in personally. Your goal in the first conversation is not to sell — it is to qualify and book. Ask:

  • "Are you currently working with another agent?"
  • "What's your timeline — are you looking to move in the next 3 months or further out?"
  • "What's your budget range?"

If they are qualified and motivated, send your Calendly link immediately: "I have a few spots open this week for a free 15-minute strategy call — here is my calendar: [link]."

Module 4

The Strategy Session (Closing the Deal)

When a lead books a call, your job is not to sell them. Your job is to diagnose their situation and prescribe a solution. The best closers are the best listeners.

The 4-Step Strategy Session Framework

  1. Discovery: "What prompted you to book the call today?" Let them talk. Do not interrupt. The more they share, the more you understand their real motivation.
  2. Timeline & Motivation: "When are you hoping to make this move? And what happens if you don't?" The second question is critical — it surfaces urgency and emotional stakes.
  3. The Prescription: "Based on what you've told me, here is the exact strategy we would use for your situation..." Be specific. Reference what they told you. Show them you listened.
  4. The Close: "Does that sound like a plan you'd like to move forward with?" If yes: "Great — let's get started. The first step is [next action]." If no: "What would need to be different for this to make sense?"

Handling Objections

ObjectionResponse
"I'm just looking right now""That's totally fine — most of my best clients started exactly where you are. The call is just to map out your options so you know what's possible. No pressure at all."
"I'm already working with an agent""I completely respect that. If things ever change or you want a second opinion on anything, I'm always here."
"The market is too uncertain""That's actually why now is a great time to get a plan in place — so when the right opportunity comes, you're ready to move fast instead of scrambling."
"I can't afford it right now""Let's figure out what you can afford and work backward from there. You might be closer than you think."

Practice the 4-step framework on your next 3 calls before your first ad campaign goes live. The system brings the leads — your job is to convert them on the call.
