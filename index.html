FAQ

Common questions.
Straight answers.

What exactly happens in the first 14 days?
We build. Days 1 through 14 are protected build time — we are inside your system configuring your CRM, AI outbound engine, branded funnel, onboarding automations, and pipelines. You do not hear from us with questions. You hear from us on Day 14 with a group reveal call where we walk your entire team through what was built, how it works, and who on your team will be the designated point person going forward. That call creates shared accountability before anyone touches the system.
When does billing start?
Your System Build Investment is due when you sign. Your system is delivered in 14 days. Monthly billing begins 30 days after you sign — giving you the full build window plus a 14-day buffer to review and confirm everything before your first invoice. Day 0: sign and pay. Day 14: system delivered. Day 30: first monthly payment due.
What is the difference between Launch and Managed after the build?
Launch is a handoff. On Day 14 we deliver the system, train your designated point person one-on-one, and your team takes ownership. We provide ongoing support through Slack and a dedicated client success contact — but your team operates the system day to day.

Managed is a kickoff. On Day 14 we show your team what was built — but nobody on your team is taking it over. We are the operators. We log in, update scripts, optimize sequences, deploy producer systems, run weekly reporting calls, and tell you what is working and what is not. Your job is to show up to the weekly call and make business decisions. That is it.
Who handles support and questions after the build?
Every client gets access to a dedicated Slack workspace and a client success contact who is in touch daily or every other day. Your team never has to wait for a response or dig through a help center. Questions go to Slack. Answers come back fast. Adam stays focused on strategy and growth — your day-to-day questions are handled by the team.
What are we responsible for as the client?
Three things: complete the onboarding intake form before Day 1 so we have everything we need to build, record your VSL video when you are ready (we give you the full script and framework — there is no deadline, your VSL page is built and waiting), and designate a point person on your team who will attend the Day 14 reveal call and be the internal owner of the system. Everything else is on us.
Are there additional costs beyond the setup and monthly fee?
Yes — and we are transparent about it. You are responsible for the platform usage costs that fuel the system: AI voice and SMS messaging (billed at competitive per-message rates through our direct carrier relationships, significantly lower than going directly to any provider), and a one-time A2P 10DLC registration fee of approximately $100–$200. These are pass-through costs, not markups. We will show you exactly what to expect before you sign.
Is LayerEngine only for real estate brokerages?
No. The six-layer framework applies to any organization that recruits producers, generates leads, and needs to retain the people they bring on. Mortgage lending teams, insurance agencies, and financial services organizations are all strong fits. If you are not sure whether your organization qualifies, book a strategy call and we will tell you honestly within the first 10 minutes.
