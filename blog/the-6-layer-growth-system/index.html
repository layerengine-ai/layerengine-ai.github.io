What is the 6-Layer Growth System?

The 6-Layer Growth System is a comprehensive architecture designed to automate the entire lifecycle of an agent within your brokerage. It moves away from fragmented tools and integrates everything into a single, cohesive engine—typically powered by advanced CRM platforms like GoHighLevel.

Layer 1: The Attraction Engine

The foundation of growth is a consistent flow of qualified candidates. The Attraction Engine leverages automated omni-channel outreach (SMS, email, voicemail drops) to engage potential recruits at scale. It replaces the manual dialer with smart, AI-driven conversations that pre-qualify candidates before they ever reach your calendar.

Layer 2: The Conversion Pipeline

Once a candidate expresses interest, they enter the Conversion Pipeline. This layer automates follow-ups, schedules appointments, and delivers targeted content (like case studies and brokerage value propositions) to warm up the recruit. It ensures no candidate falls through the cracks due to human error.

"Before implementing a structured system, our recruiting was feast or famine. We were either making calls or doing interviews, never both. The 6-Layer approach allowed us to keep the pipeline full automatically, so I could focus purely on closing the best agents."

Layer 3: Seamless Onboarding

The first 30 days dictate an agent's long-term success. Layer 3 automates the entire onboarding process—from document signing and compliance checks to tech stack setup and initial training. This provides a VIP experience for the new agent while saving your management team dozens of hours per hire.

Layer 4: Done-For-You Lead Generation

To retain agents, you must help them make money quickly. Layer 4 provides agents with a turnkey lead generation system. By offering pre-built funnels, automated follow-up campaigns, and integrated lead sources, you give your agents an immediate competitive advantage.

Layer 5: The Retention Dashboard

You can't manage what you don't measure. The Retention Dashboard tracks agent performance, engagement, and satisfaction metrics in real-time. It uses predictive analytics to flag at-risk agents before they start looking for the exit, allowing you to intervene proactively.

Layer 6: The Advocacy Loop

The final layer turns your successful agents into your best recruiters. By automating referral requests and capturing success stories, the Advocacy Loop feeds social proof back into Layer 1, creating a self-sustaining growth cycle.
