What is A2P 10DLC?

A2P 10DLC stands for Application-to-Person 10-Digit Long Code. In plain English, it refers to a system established by major US mobile carriers (like AT&T, Verizon, and T-Mobile) to regulate text messages sent from businesses (applications) to consumers (persons) using standard 10-digit local phone numbers.

The goal of A2P 10DLC is simple: protect consumers from spam while providing legitimate businesses with better delivery rates and higher throughput. If you want to use SMS to recruit agents or loan officers, you must register your business and your messaging campaigns with the carriers. Failing to do so means your messages will be heavily filtered, blocked entirely, or subject to steep fines.

Why This Matters for Real Estate Recruiting

Recruiting is a numbers game, but it's also a relationship game. SMS is the most effective way to cut through the noise and reach potential recruits directly on their most personal device. However, if your messages aren't getting delivered because you're not compliant, you're burning through your lead lists and wasting money.

How to Achieve A2P 10DLC Compliance

Getting compliant involves a few key steps. It requires working with your CRM or messaging provider (like GoHighLevel or Twilio) to submit your business details to The Campaign Registry (TCR), a third-party organization that handles A2P 10DLC registration for the carriers.

  • Brand Registration: You must verify your business identity by providing your EIN, legal business name, address, and website.
  • Campaign Registration: You must describe exactly what type of messages you will be sending (e.g., "Real Estate Agent Recruiting") and provide sample messages.
  • Opt-In/Opt-Out Mechanisms: You must clearly demonstrate how recipients consent to receive messages (opt-in) and how they can stop them (replying STOP).
  • Trust Score Assignment: Based on your brand details, you will be assigned a "Trust Score" which determines your message throughput limits.

The Bottom Line: A2P 10DLC compliance isn't just about avoiding penalties; it's about guaranteeing that your recruiting messages actually reach the top-producing agents you want to hire. In a crowded market, the brokerage that gets their message delivered is the brokerage that wins.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Do I need A2P 10DLC registration if I only text a few agents a day?

Yes. If you are using an application (like a CRM or automated dialer) to send texts from a 10-digit number for business purposes, you must be registered, regardless of the volume.

How long does the A2P 10DLC registration process take?

The timeline can vary depending on your provider and the accuracy of the information you submit. It typically takes anywhere from a few days to a few weeks for campaigns to be fully vetted and approved.

Can I just use my personal cell phone to recruit instead?

While you can use your personal phone (which is P2P, or Person-to-Person messaging), it is not scalable for a growing brokerage. You cannot automate follow-ups, integrate with your CRM, or track metrics effectively without an A2P system.
